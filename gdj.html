About GDJ (Global Developer's Jam Club)

  • GDJ Club is a student organization at the National Higher School of AI (ENSIA) that promotes coding, software development, and innovation.
  • We aim to bridge creativity and technology, helping students develop apps, games, and web projects.
  • Founded in 2024, GDJ organizes hackathons, workshops, coding challenges, and networking events to connect students with professionals and companies.
  • Our mission is to empower developers, encourage innovation, and foster teamwork within the ENSIA community.
  • Whether you are a coder, designer, or entrepreneur, GDJ is the place to bring ideas to life.
  • GDJ — Code. Create. Connect.
