About Skill & Tell Club

  • Skill & Tell Club is a scientific and cultural club at the ENSIA.
  • It focuses on developing both technical and soft skills, helping students grow personally and professionally through interactive activities.
  • The club aims to bridge communication, teamwork, and creativity with technology.
  • Through training sessions, workshops, and events, Skill & Tell empowers students to gain confidence and leadership skills.
  • Skill & Tell — Where Skills Meet Expression.
