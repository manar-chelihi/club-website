About ESCC (ENSIA Sport & Culture Club)

  • ESCC (ENSIA Sport & Culture Club) is the vibrant student club at ENSIA dedicated to promoting sports, culture, and holistic student life.
  • It serves as a bridge between physical activity, artistic expression and community building on campus.
  • Founded in 2023, ESCC organises sports tournaments, music & arts events, cultural festivals and wellness workshops.
  • Its mission is to enhance student wellbeing, foster cultural diversity, and build a strong sense of belonging within the ENSIA community.
  • Whether you’re into sports, music, theatre, or cultural leadership, ESCC is the place to play, create and connect.
  • ESCC — Move. Inspire. Belong.
