About SecAI


  • SecAI (Science & Artificial Intelligence Club) is one of the scientific clubs at the National Higher School of Artificial Intelligence (ENSIA).

  • It focuses on artificial intelligence, research, and innovation, providing students with opportunities to apply theory through hands-on projects.

  • Through hackathons, workshops, and scientific events such as Hack&Train, SecAI fosters a culture of creativity, problem-solving, and collaboration.

  • Its mission is to advance scientific thinking, encourage research, and connect students with industry and institutions.

  • Whether you are passionate about AI, data science, or innovation, SecAI is the community where curiosity meets creation.
