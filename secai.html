About SecAI

  • SecAI (Science & Artificial Intelligence Club) is a scientific club at ENSIA.
  • It focuses on artificial intelligence, research, and innovation, providing students with hands-on projects.
  • Through hackathons, workshops, and events such as Hack&Train, SecAI encourages creativity and collaboration.
  • Its mission is to advance scientific thinking and connect students with industry.
  • Whether you are passionate about AI, data science, or innovation, SecAI is the community where curiosity meets creation.
  • SecAI — Shaping the Future through Science and AI.
