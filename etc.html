About ETC Club

  • ETC Club is an engineering and technology club at ENSIA, bringing together passionate students from diverse technical backgrounds.
  • It focuses on electronics, robotics, programming, AI, IoT, and innovative tech solutions to prepare members for real-world challenges.
  • The club organizes hands-on workshops, coding competitions, robotics contests, hackathons, and collaborative projects to develop practical skills and teamwork.
  • Members have opportunities to engage with industry experts, attend tech conferences, and work on research-oriented projects, bridging the gap between academia and industry.
  • Its mission is to develop technical talent, foster innovation, encourage creativity, and promote collaboration among students.
  • The club also emphasizes community outreach, mentorship, and interdisciplinary learning to inspire future engineers and innovators.
  • ETC — Explore. Transform. Create.
