About Skill & Tell


  • Skill & Tell Club is a scientific and cultural club at the National Higher School of Artificial Intelligence (ENSIA).

  • It focuses on developing both technical and soft skills, helping students grow personally and professionally through interactive activities.

  • The club aims to bridge communication, teamwork, and creativity with technology, ensuring members learn how to express and present their knowledge effectively.

  • Through training sessions, workshops, and events, Skill & Tell empowers students to gain confidence and leadership skills while exploring areas like design, marketing, and media production.

  • Its mission is to help ENSIA students develop essential skills needed in the modern workplace — from communication and teamwork to creativity and innovation.

  • Skill & Tell — Where Skills Meet Expression.
