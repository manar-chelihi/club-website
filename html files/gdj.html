About GDJ (Google Developer Juniors)

  • GDJ (Google Developer Juniors) is a student tech club at the National Higher School of Artificial Intelligence (ENSIA).
  • It aims to inspire innovation and build a strong developer community through learning, collaboration, and real-world projects.
  • Founded in 2023, GDJ provides members with hands-on experience in Google technologies, programming, and open-source development.
  • Its mission is to empower young developers to explore the world of technology, share knowledge, and solve real problems.
  • Whether you are curious about coding or already a developer, GDJ is where you can learn, build, and grow.
