About ETC (ENSIA Tech Community)

  • ETC (ENSIA Tech Community) is the first scientific club at the National Higher School of Artificial Intelligence (ENSIA).
  • It serves as a hub for innovation in artificial intelligence, coding, robotics, and design.
  • Founded in March 2022, ETC provides hands-on learning experiences through workshops, hackathons, and competitions.
  • Its mission is to empower students, foster collaboration, and encourage professional growth.
  • Whether you are a beginner or an expert, ETC is the place where your tech journey begins.
