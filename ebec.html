About EBEC (ENSIA Business & Entrepreneurship Club)

  • EBEC (ENSIA Business & Entrepreneurship Club) is a student organization at the National Higher School of Artificial Intelligence (ENSIA) that promotes entrepreneurship and innovation.
  • It serves as a bridge between technology and business, helping students turn ideas into impactful startup projects.
  • Founded in 2023, EBEC organizes workshops, startup challenges, fairs, and networking events that connect students with professionals and companies.
  • Its mission is to empower future entrepreneurs, encourage creativity, and foster leadership within the ENSIA community.
  • Whether you dream of launching a startup or learning business skills, EBEC is the place to ignite your entrepreneurial spirit.
  • EBEC — Inspire. Build. Lead.
